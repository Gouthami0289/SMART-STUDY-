About the Platform

Study Schedule Optimization

This platform allows students to input their courses, assignments, and exam dates. It will generate optimized study schedules based on assignment deadlines, ensuring students can manage their time more effectively.

Pomodoro Timer

We have implemented a simple Pomodoro timer that helps students break their study sessions into manageable intervals, with short breaks in between. This method improves focus and productivity.

Progress Tracking

The platform tracks completion of study sessions, providing students with progress insights. You can view your past study sessions, check how many tasks are completed, and analyze your productivity over time.

Reminder System

Stay on track with built-in reminders for upcoming study sessions and tasks. The platform sends notifications so you never miss a study session or deadline.

Sharing with Study Groups

Students can share their study schedules with friends or study groups. This feature encourages collaboration and group study while keeping everyone organized and on the same page.

Task Prioritization

A simple task prioritization system allows students to focus on the most important tasks first, making sure urgent assignments are completed on time.

Analytics Dashboard

Get an overview of your study patterns and productivity through our basic analytics dashboard. Track your study hours, task completion rates, and gain insights into your habits to improve future performance.

Designed to help you study smarter and achieve your goals!
